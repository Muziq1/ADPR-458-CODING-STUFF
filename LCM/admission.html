Daily Admission

We're so excited to have you come play, so much is new you'll want to stay all day!

And you're in just the right place to get the party started...online ticket purchases are encouraged to ensure we can get you checked in and having fun as quickly as possible upon your arrival to the museum. Before you purchase there are few things we'd like you to know:

  • All tickets are non-refundable and non-transferrable however tickets can be used on any date/time the Museum is open to the public.
  • Weapons Policy: The Lincoln Children's Museum prohibits firearms, knives, or weapons of any kind.
  • For the safety and security of all guests, all adults aged 16 or older may not enter the museum unless accompanied by an infant or by a child aged 12 years or younger. All infants and children must be accompanied by an adult for the entirety of the museum visit. If visiting the museum as a larger group, we require that a 1:5 adult to child/infant ratio is met for the visit.
